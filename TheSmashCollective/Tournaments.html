Tournament Mentality

As a communitive entity, we do not openly contribute economical funds towards any particular competitive instance, event, or organization in order to to maintain neutrality and an objective perspective in community involvement. We do condone and recommend attendence, participation, assistance, management, discussion, and overall involvement in any open and honest competition on an individualistic level, without regard to the organizor(s), fellow competitor(s), commentator(s), stage assistance(s), and the remaining attendee(s). We recognize the following competitive formats:

  • Friendlies
  • Local Events
  • Regionals
  • Major Events

~The cooperative
