Cooperative

The philosophy of the Smash Cooperative is to provide a medium for exchange of benefits in for the competitive community of the gaming series, Smash Bros, with a prioritizational emphasis on Melee. We are not structured to replace any in-place functions, but to broaden different utilizations for the community.
